The Applicant now seeks a judicial review of the Minister's decision to deny its request for the cancellation or waiver of interest and penalties related to its failure to remit outstanding CPP contributions for the 2001 2002 and 2003 taxation years.The case involves the Applicant's request for relief from the Canada Revenue Agency's (CRA) decision not to cancel the penalty imposed for failing to remit the Canada Pension Plan (CPP) contributions for its employees. The applicant's request was denied by a CRA Officer and upheld by the Fairness Committee. The issue is whether the Minister's decision was correct. The standard of review is not specified.
