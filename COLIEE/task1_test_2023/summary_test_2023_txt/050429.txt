Apotex Inc. requested an order to reconsider the terms of an order issued on September 25 1997 which granted them the right to cross-examine on the reply affidavits. Rule 337(5)(b) of the Federal Court Rules was cited and Apotex Inc. lists 14 grounds for bringing the present notice of motion before the court. The request for extension of time was granted and Apotex Inc. is permitted to file reply evidence within seven days.
