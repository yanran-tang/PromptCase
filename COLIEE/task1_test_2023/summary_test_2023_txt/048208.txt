The case involves a dispute over the validity of reassessments made by the Minister of National Revenue in relation to the defendant's income tax liability for the 1979-1981 taxation years. The defendant filed returns for those years on the basis that his wife was involved in the farming operation and that it was a partnership. The Minister reassessed the defendant on the basis of reallocation of income under a repealed provision of the Income Tax Act but later confirmed the reassessments on the basis that the farming operation was a sole proprietorship.
