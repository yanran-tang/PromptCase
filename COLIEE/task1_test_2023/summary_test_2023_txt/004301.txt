La demande de contrôle a été rejetée.The Canadian Federal Court has rejected a citizen of Russia's request for a judicial review of the negative finding by an immigration officer on his visa exemption application based on humanitarian and compassionate considerations. The applicant had previously filed a refugee claim which was denied and his pre-removal risk assessment was refused. The Court determined that the officer's decision was reasonable and the applicant failed to provide sufficient evidence to support his application. The decision was based on the applicant's departure from Canada not causing him or his family undue hardship.
