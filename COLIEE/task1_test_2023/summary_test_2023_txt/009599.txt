Lizbeth Adriana Gomez Espinoza her mother and brother applied for refugee status in Canada claiming a fear of persecution by the principal applicant's ex-partner a drug trafficker in Mexico. The Refugee Protection Division rejected their claim stating that they had a viable Internal Flight Alternative in Guadalajara Mexico. The applicants sought judicial review of this decision but it was dismissed by the Federal Court. The court found that the Board had adequately considered the issue of state protection and concluded that the applicants had a viable IFA in Guadalajara therefore falling within acceptable outcomes.
