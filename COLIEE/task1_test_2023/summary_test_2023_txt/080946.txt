The case involves an applicant from China who fled to Canada after his wife was forced to have an abortion due to a violation of China's family planning policy. The applicant sought to quash the Convention Refugee Determination Division's decision and compel a new hearing claiming that the tribunal misunderstood the definition of a refugee acted beyond its jurisdiction denied a fair hearing and made erroneous findings of fact. However the court found no evidence of bias or procedural irregularities and ultimately dismissed the application.
