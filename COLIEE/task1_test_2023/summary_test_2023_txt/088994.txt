This is the story of Azucena Margarita Ramos Ramirez and her four children two of whom were born in Canada one in El Salvador and one in the United States. Ramirez faces deportation with her youngest daughter Jessie but filed a humanitarian and compassionate application that was refused. After a review it was determined that the officer's decision was not reasonable and Ramirez was granted leave to review the H&C decision.A Salvadoran family attempted to seek refugee status in Canada after experiencing poor living conditions and gang violence in El Salvador. They were ineligible due to illegally entering Canada but filed an H&C application citing the risk of future harassment and separation from family. The application was denied on the grounds of available state protection and an internal flight alternative in El Salvador.
