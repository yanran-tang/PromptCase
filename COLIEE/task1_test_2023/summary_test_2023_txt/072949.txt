Ricardo Chacon Colindres a citizen of Honduras filed for judicial review of a Pre-Removal Risk Assessment (PRRA) officer's decision in which his PRRA was dismissed. The officer concluded that the application did not show that the applicant was at risk of torture cruel and unusual treatment punishment or death if he returned to Honduras. The applicant claimed that the officer erred in his assessment of evidence and state protection. The court found that the PRRA officer's decision was reasonable and did not infringe the Charter or the Convention against Torture and Other Cruel Inhuman or Degrading Treatment or Punishment.
