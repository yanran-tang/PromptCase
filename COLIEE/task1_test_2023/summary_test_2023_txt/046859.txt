The applicant a citizen of Brazil who had been subjected to domestic violence at the hands of her husband was denied refugee status by the Convention Refugee Determination Division (CRDD) of the Immigration and Refugee Board. The CRDD found that the applicant's fear of persecution did not fall under any of the grounds for Convention refugee status. The applicant appealed the decision and a judge concluded that women subject to domestic violence in Brazil constitute a particular social group and referred the matter back to the CRDD for rehearing and redetermination.
