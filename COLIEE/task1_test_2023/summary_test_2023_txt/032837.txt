The case involves a dispute over the leadership selection process of the Matchewan Customary Council chosen to represent the Algonquins of Barriere Lake. The Application for Judicial Review was discontinued on March 5 2010 and the respondent filed its Bill of Costs requesting an assessment of costs. The assessment officer allows the respondent's claims for costs stating that there was no public interest at issue in the matter and disallowing the claimed PST on assessable services.
