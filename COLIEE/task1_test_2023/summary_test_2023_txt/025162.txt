The plaintiff a convicted drug trafficker filed an application for judicial review challenging the decisions made by the Director of the Regional Reception Centre to set his security rating at medium and order his transfer to a medium security penitentiary. He also challenged the decision that he was not eligible for the accelerated review procedure. However the court dismissed his application for judicial review on the grounds that he missed the deadline for the decision regarding the accelerated parole review and he did not exhaust other remedies such as the grievance procedure.
