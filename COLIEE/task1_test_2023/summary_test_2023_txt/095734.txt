The plaintiff seeks to stay the action pending the disposition of the appeal from an order or an alternatively stay the defendant's motion for summary judgment filed on the grounds of the passing off trademark infringement unfair competition and conversion. The defendants are Infinity Investment Counsel Ltd. and Infinity Funds Management Inc. who have brought a motion under the Federal Court Rules for the summary judgment dismissing all the claims in the statement of claim. The court denied the motion for a stay of proceedings and the costs of the motion are awarded to the defendants.
