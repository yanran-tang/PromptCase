The applicant seeks to file a reply affidavit to answer an affidavit filed by George Kolk. While the respondents are willing to allow the applicant to cross-examine Kolk they want it to be done in Ottawa which the applicant is unable to do. The respondents are unwilling to consent to the filing of a reply affidavit without seeing its content first. The court grants permission to file a reply affidavit and the respondents can object to its content during the hearing.
