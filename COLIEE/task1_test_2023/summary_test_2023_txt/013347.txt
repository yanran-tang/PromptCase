The applicants are seeking certiorari and mandamus to quash and compel the decision of the Department of External Affairs regarding the sponsorship of Convention Refugees from Indochina for permanent residence in Canada. The applicants have been refused based on lack of required units of assessment and lack of demand for their type of employment. They have obtained exit visas from Vietnam and a guarantee of employment for two of the applicants. The combined income of the household will be approximately $47000 per annum.The applicant's request for her siblings to come to Canada was denied due to their lack of skills or knowledge of official languages making it difficult for them to establish themselves in Canada. The applicant's affidavit includes letters from the Canadian Embassy in Bangkok and the Minister of State for Immigration confirming the denial of her siblings' applications and a successful application for approval of her undertaking to assist their landing in Canada.
