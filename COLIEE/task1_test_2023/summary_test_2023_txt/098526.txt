Javier Ivan Torres Rubianes a citizen of Colombia claims to have a well-founded fear of persecution by the guerrilla groups because of his perceived political opinion. Rubianes filed an application for judicial review of a decision of the Refugee Protection Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board which found that Rubianes is excluded from entitlement to protection due to his alleged complicity in crimes against humanity. The Federal Court dismissed Rubianes' application after considering the factors of the test for complicity.
