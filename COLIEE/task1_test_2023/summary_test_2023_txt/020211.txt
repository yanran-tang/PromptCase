This case concerns an application for a permanent resident visa to Canada by a citizen of Oman which was rejected due to insufficient details about his employment. The applicant filed for judicial review of the decision but it was dismissed. The case offers a detailed account of the applicant's work experience and the documents required by the immigration officer.A Federal Court of Canada case reviews an immigration officer’s decision to reject an applicant for Canada’s Federal Skilled Worker program. The applicant failed to provide sufficient details about his employment as a pilot. The court applies a reasonableness standard to review the officer’s decision and determines that the officer did not fetter her discretion and the decision was reasonable.
