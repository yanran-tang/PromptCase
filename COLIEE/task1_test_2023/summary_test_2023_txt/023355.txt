The case involves a Ghanaian citizen seeking refugee protection but on the basis of his involvement in a repressive terrorist group the Committee for the Defence of the Revolution (CDR) the applicant admitted to participating in crimes against human rights and was excluded from refugee protection under Article 1F(a) of the United Nations Convention Relating to the Status of Refugees. The applicant claimed he was spying on the group but the Refugee Board did not find this a sufficient justification to absolve him of complicity in the CDR's actions.
