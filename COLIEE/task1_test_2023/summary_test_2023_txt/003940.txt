The applicant seeks costs for detention which was carried out illegally by the respondent who detained the applicant for removal despite the existence of a probation order which barred the removal of the applicant. The respondent's officials ignored the law and the adjudicator was misled by their counsel. The court finds that there were special reasons for awarding costs to the applicant. The costs incurred were due to the actions of the respondent and the court orders the respondent to pay costs accordingly.
