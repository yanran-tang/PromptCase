The defendant Her Majesty the Queen has requested security for costs in the amount of $10000 from the plaintiff who filed an action claiming he was defamed and maliciously slandered in an illegal selection process for a project manager position at the Department of Health and Welfare. The defendant seeks security for costs under subsections 416(1)(e) and (f) of the Federal Court Rules. The defendant is entitled to an order for security for costs pursuant to paragraph 416(1)(f) since the evidence produced by Her on this motion meets all of the conditions for the application of that paragraph.
