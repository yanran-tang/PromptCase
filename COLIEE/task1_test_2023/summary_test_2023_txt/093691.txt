The case involves a claim of copyright infringement and a counterclaim. Mr. Ruggles claims copyright in advertising material and in a video that he prepared to sell a block of Fording Coal Ltd condominium units at Elkford. Defendants used the material and Mr. Ruggles sued both Fording and Ms. Paulhus. However he later decided to discontinue the action against Ms. Paulhus. This ruling concerns Ms. Paulhus's claim for costs after Mr. Ruggles discontinued the action against her. The court decides that she is entitled to document discovery preparation for examination and counsel's attendance on discovery costing a total of $1000 plus preparation of the summary judgment motion for $600.
