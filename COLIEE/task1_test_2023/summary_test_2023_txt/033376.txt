Mr. Randhawa a citizen of India and experienced assistant cook specializing in North Indian cuisine applied for a work permit to work at a Toronto restaurant. The visa officer refused the application citing concerns about Mr. Randhawa's ability to maintain proper hygiene in the kitchen based on an assessment interview. The court ruled that the visa officer's decision was unreasonable and lacked objective measures of Mr. Randhawa's fitness for the job and allowed Mr. Randhawa's application for judicial review.
