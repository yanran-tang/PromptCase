The article discusses Nadeem Akhter Ali's claim for refugee status in Canada as he had fled from Pakistan after being targeted by the MQM (Muttahida Quami Movement) political party. The Immigration and Refugee Board (IRB) dismissed Ali's claim stating that he did not have a well-founded fear of persecution and was excluded under Article 1F(a) of the United Nations Convention Relating to the Status of Refugees.
