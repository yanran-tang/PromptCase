Mr. Bi a resident of Hong Kong was denied permanent residence in Canada and his application was rejected on September 9th 1996. The visa officer advised him that he was entitled to a refund of the Right of Landing Fee which was being processed. However by July 16th 1997 he had not received the refund which led to his case being reconsidered. The visa officer responded on July 25th 1997 rejecting Mr. Bi's application for permanent residency and triggering an application for judicial review.
