The ministerial delegate denied his claim citing a lack of evidence. MacDonald requested a review of the decision but it was again denied. The case was returned to the Board as their determination lacked proper analysis.Mr. MacDonald appealed a decision made by the Board regarding his eligibility for a pension under the VRAB Act based on his service in the RCMP. The Board concluded that there was no evidence to support his claims related to foot and nasal conditions. The judicial review concluded that the Board's decision was reasonable and that Mr. MacDonald failed to prove his entitlement to a pension.
