The defendant Hargrave applied to strike out the Statement of Claim made by Mr. Steiner which alleged that the police were set on him by the Crown following a motion made in a prior action that was dismissed. The defendant claimed that the present action was scandalous frivolous vexatious and disclosed no reasonable cause of action. The court found that the action lacked a reasonable cause of action and that it was scandalous and frivolous and thus struck it out.
