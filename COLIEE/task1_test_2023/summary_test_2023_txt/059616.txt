The Federal Court rejected the request for judicial review of Javier Fernando Monzon Ortega Diana Marcela Barriga Perez Daniel Fernando Monzon Barriga Diana Alejandra Monzon Barriga and others who were denied refugee status in Canada by the Immigration and Refugee Board. The applicants claimed that the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia threatened them due to their possession of confidential information. However the RPD determined that the FARC's influence had decreased and that the state protection was available. The court found the RPD's decision reasonable and dismissed the application.
