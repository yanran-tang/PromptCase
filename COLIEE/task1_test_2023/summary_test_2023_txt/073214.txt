The applicant a citizen of Sri Lanka was determined not to be a Convention refugee by the Immigration and Refugee Board due to an adverse finding of credibility regarding his involvement with the New Democratic United National Front (DUNF). The applicant argues that the Board breached natural justice by accepting his testimony during the hearing but later impeaching it in their decision. The court finds that the Board gave the applicant a false impression and breached natural justice allowing the application for judicial review and returning it for rehearing and reconsideration.
