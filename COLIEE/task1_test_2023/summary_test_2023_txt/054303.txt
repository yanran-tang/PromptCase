The plaintiff appealed an order to provide security for costs which was granted by the Prothonotary. The plaintiff owed court costs of $4750 as a result of orders dismissing applications and had not paid any of them. The Prothonotary granted an order for security of costs of $10000 up to and including examination for discovery. The plaintiff appealed the order on the basis of four grounds but the appeal was dismissed by the Federal Court Mandamin.
