This case involves an application for judicial review of a decision by the National Parole Board to deny the applicant day and full parole. The applicant has been in prison since 1977 and was previously granted parole in 1992 and 2003 but had it revoked in 1996 and 2004. The applicant disputes allegations of drug-related misconduct and challenges the withholding of certain security intelligence reports from him.The Board denied the applicant's requests for parole based on past conduct drug trafficking a negative attitude towards his Case Management Team (CMT) and a lack of progress made between the applicant and his CMT. The applicant appealed the decision but the Appeal Division affirmed the Board's decision. The applicant now challenges the validity of the decision claiming a lack of communication from the Board.
