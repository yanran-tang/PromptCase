This case is an application for judicial review of a decision made by the Refugee Protection Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board where the applicant claimed to be homosexual and at risk in Uganda. The Board rejected the claim because the applicant failed to establish his identity. The applicant arrived in Canada using travel documents belonging to someone else and was detained until August 2011 then again from October 2011 until February 2012.ound that the documents submitted by the applicant including a student identity card and a birth certificate were of poor quality and contained no security features casting doubt on their authenticity. A Ugandan passport was also found to have been altered and improperly obtained. The applicant's identity could not be established leading the Board to reject his claim for protection.
