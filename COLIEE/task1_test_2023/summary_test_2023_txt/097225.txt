The plaintiff a member of the Sakimay Indian Band claimed that the defendants breached their constitutional and fiduciary obligations including discriminatory acts towards her. The defendant Sakimay Indian Band Council sought to strike the statement of claim arguing that the relief sought was of the type contemplated by s. 18 of the Federal Court Act and could only be obtained by way of an application for judicial review. The court struck the portions of the statement of claim seeking prerogative relief and maintained the claim for damages.
