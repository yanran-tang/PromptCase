Selwyn Pieters an employee with the Immigration and Refugee Board filed a complaint of workplace harassment under the Treasury Board's Policy on the Prevention and Resolution of Harassment in the Workplace and filed a number of grievances under the Collective Agreement against other employees. Pieters also filed an application for judicial review seeking disclosure of the details of a harassment complaint against him. The Court ruled that its jurisdiction was ousted by the complete code for resolution of workplace disputes set out in the Collective Agreement and the application was dismissed without leave to amend.
