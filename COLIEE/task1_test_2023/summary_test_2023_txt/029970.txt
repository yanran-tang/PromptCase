The Minister of Human Resources Development seeks judicial review of a decision by a member of the Pension Appeals Board to grant Mr de Tommaso leave to appeal seven years after the time for doing so had elapsed. The Applicant argues that the Respondent failed to meet the factors required for an extension of time including showing a continuing intention to pursue the appeal an arguable case reasonable explanation for the delay and no resulting prejudice to the opposing party. The court grants the application and sets aside the decision remitting the matter for re-determination.
