The applicant a Shia Muslim from Pakistan claimed refugee status in Canada alleging that he was attacked numerous times by a Sunni extremist group and that he was no longer safe in Pakistan. The Refugee Protection Division rejected his claim finding aspects of his story not to be credible and concluding that adequate state protection existed in Pakistan. The applicant sought judicial review of this decision challenging findings of fact and credibility. The court denied his application finding that the Board did not err in its conclusions.
