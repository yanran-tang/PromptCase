The case involves the judicial review of a decision by the Refugee Protection Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board. The applicant a citizen of Colombia claimed to be a victim of violence extortion and death threats by the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia (FARC). The RPD rejected her claim but the court found two reviewable errors in this decision. The matter was referred back to the IRB for reconsideration before a different member of the Board's Refugee Protection Division.
