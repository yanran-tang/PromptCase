The applicant an Israeli citizen left Israel with her daughter in 1992 after experiencing discrimination harassment assault and threats due to her membership in a particular social group religion and nationality. She claimed refugee status in Canada but was denied by the Refugee Division which found her experiences to be problems associated with settling into a new country. However the Federal Court found that her experiences amounted to persecution and that she could not obtain state protection in Israel. The matter was referred back for a re-hearing.
