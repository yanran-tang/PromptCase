The Professional Institute of the Public Service of Canada (PIPSC) is seeking judicial review of the Canadian Museum of Nature's decision to withhold a forensic accounting report prepared for the Museum's Board of Trustees. The report was ordered to investigate allegations made in a report published by the PIPSC's union committee which criticised the Museum's management and handling of funds.
