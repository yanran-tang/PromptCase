This case involves an appeal under subsection 14(5) of the Citizenship Act from a decision of a Citizenship Judge denying the applicant's application for citizenship on the basis that he failed to establish residency. The applicant a citizen of Iran was required to have accumulated 1095 days of residency in Canada during the previous four years but failed to provide adequate evidence to prove his physical presence in Canada during the relevant period resulting in the Judge denying his application. The court found that the Judge correctly applied the law and weighed all of the evidence dismissing the application for judicial review.
