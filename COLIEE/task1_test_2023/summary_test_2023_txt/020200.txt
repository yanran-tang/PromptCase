This case involves an application for permanent residency in Canada by a skilled worker Mr. Oladipo who failed to provide reliable documentation regarding his employment history in the United States and Nigeria. The Visa Officer denied his application and Mr. Oladipo sought judicial review. The court upheld the decision to deny his application citing his failure to meet the necessary criteria and provide sufficient documentation.Mr. Oladipo's application for permanent residence in Canada was refused as the visa officer was not satisfied with the documentation provided to support his work experience in the US and Nigeria. The officer also questioned his credibility due to the lack of reliable documentation. Mr. Oladipo claims that the officer erred in considering his illegal employment in the US and failing to explain the rejection of his Nigerian work experience. The Respondent contends that the officer denied the application due to credibility issues and lack of reliable documentation.
