Dee M. Babin the applicant sought to waive the penalties and interest imposed on her 2001 income tax return by Canada Customs and Revenue Agency (CCRA) the respondent. The CCRA refused to waive the penalties and interest payable on Babin's income tax return. The case went for judicial review and the CCRA's decision was upheld as Babin was responsible for ensuring her 2001 income tax return was correctly filed on time.
