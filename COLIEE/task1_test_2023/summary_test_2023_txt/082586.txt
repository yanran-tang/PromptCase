The plaintiff brings a motion to compel the defendant to file a list of documents and an affidavit certifying such list pursuant to rule 448 of the Federal Court Rules. The plaintiff's claim is for damages arising out of the rescinding of a $400000 grant awarded to the plaintiff under the Industrial Regional Development Programme 1 or specific performance of the grant agreement. The court grants the plaintiff's motion for a rule 448 order despite the defendant's objections.
