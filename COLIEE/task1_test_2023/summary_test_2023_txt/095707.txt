Nubia Del Carmen Orellana Ortega a citizen of El Salvador sought refuge in Canada in 2010 due to the risk of harm from the Mara 18 gang which had kidnapped beaten raped and extorted money from her. However the Immigration and Refugee Board rejected her claim and her application for judicial review was dismissed in 2012 despite her insistence that the Board had misinterpreted her statements and undervalued the severity of gang violence in El Salvador.
