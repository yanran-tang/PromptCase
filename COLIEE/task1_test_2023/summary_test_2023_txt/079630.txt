Jose Noli Delos Santos a citizen of the Philippines sought judicial review of the refusal of his application for an inland visa exemption under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act. The Officer rejected his application as it lacked new evidence and considered his severe misrepresentations to his marital and parental status upon entering Canada. The applicant argued that the Officer was biased as they considered both his H&C and PRRA applications. The Federal Court dismissed the applicant's application for judicial review as there was no proof of bias or unreasonable exercise of discretion by the officer.
