A legal case involves an application pursuant to Rule 465(18) for Edward Zyblut and Gordon McFarlane to answer specific questions asked during an examination for discovery. The case concerns the validity of a fisheries program with questions seeking facts and relevant documents to the case. The court orders relevant questions to be answered and documents produced within 20 days along with the filing of the examination transcripts and costs in the cause. Some questions seeking opinions and irrelevant questions need not be answered.
