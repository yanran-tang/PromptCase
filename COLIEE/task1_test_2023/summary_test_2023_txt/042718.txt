The case concerns provisions of Canadian law regarding immigration and territory denial to criminals or security risks. The minister of public safety can ban a foreign national on the basis of a detailed report. The immigration section can impose special measures for humanitarian reasons or suspend a deportation order in certain cases. The text includes a summary of the relevant provisions of the law.This case involves a judicial review of a decision by the Immigration Appeal Division (IAD) to grant a stay of a removal order against a Salvadorian citizen who had been convicted of various offences. The Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness is challenging the IAD's decision and the case is being sent back to the IAD for a new review.
