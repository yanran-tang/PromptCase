The applicant seeks to strike out the Lachance affidavit and exhibits in response to the application for judicial review as they were not before the decision-maker. The court determines that the affidavit contains substantial information that could be relevant to the case and grants the applicant leave to file an affidavit in reply with both parties allowed to cross-examine the affiants. The court deems it in the interest of justice not to strike out the Lachance affidavit and exhibits.
