Therefore the Court dismissed Mr. Latham's application for judicial review.In a case regarding a prisoner's conditional release discrepancies in his criminal record from Australia were brought up. The prisoner argued that the documentation was unreliable and should not have been used but the Appeal Division found that it was credible. The prisoner also claimed that his record contained inaccurate psychiatric reports but the division found no error in the decision to deny his release. The division also considered his past failures while under supervision and his difficulties with correctional staff as reasons for denial.
