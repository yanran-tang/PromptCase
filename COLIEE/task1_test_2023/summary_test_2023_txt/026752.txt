The applicants Astra and Takeda requested directions to the assessment officer regarding the assessment of costs in a case against Genpharm. They sought an order for directions in accordance with the highest end of column V of Tariff B. The judge directed that costs should be assessed at the upper end of column III of Tariff B and allowed Astra's request for second counsel fees but denied Takeda's request. The judge also allowed Takeda to claim economy class airfare between Ottawa and Toronto as a travel expense.
