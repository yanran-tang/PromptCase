Dr. Shiv Chopra an employee of the Department of National Health and Welfare filed a grievance alleging discrimination based on his ethnic origin under the PIPSC Master Agreement. The grievance was referred to adjudication but the adjudicator determined that he did not have jurisdiction to consider it.
