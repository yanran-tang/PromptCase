Shahin Kashani Aghdam an Iranian citizen who arrived in Canada in 1986 sought an order of mandamus to compel the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness to make a decision regarding her application for Ministerial relief which has been outstanding for six years. The Federal Court agreed with Aghdam and ordered the Minister to make a decision within 120 days of the receipt of Aghdam's submissions with costs fixed at $3500.
