The case involves a motion by China Ocean Shipping and Guangzhou Ocean Shipping to strike out the in rem portion of Paramount Enterprises International's claim relating to an arrested ship and cargo. Paramount accuses the defendants of breaching a Conlinebooking charter party by transporting the cargo on a ship belonging to the defendants causing financial losses amounting to approximately $175000. The court determines that Paramount cannot take action against the ship and cargo as they are not the subject of the dispute and the defendants' motion to strike out Paramount's claim is allowed.
